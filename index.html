What You'll Create

Espresso-Based Drinks

Espresso

The foundation — a concentrated 30ml shot pulled at 9 bar pressure with rich crema.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water — bold, clean, and endlessly customisable.

Latte

Espresso with silky steamed milk and a delicate layer of microfoam.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and thick velvety foam — a classic balance.

Flat White

Double ristretto with velvety microfoam — smaller, stronger, and more intense.

Mocha

Espresso meets premium chocolate and steamed milk — indulgent and comforting.

Curriculum

What You'll Explore

01

Coffee Origin & Processing

Explore how growing region, altitude, and processing method shape the character of your cup.

02

Grind Size & Extraction

Understand the relationship between grind coarseness and extraction yield for balanced flavour.

03

Brewing Ratio

Learn the golden ratio and how adjusting coffee-to-water ratios impacts strength and body.

04

V60 / Filter Coffee Techniques

Hands-on practice with Hario V60, bloom technique, pour patterns, and flow rate control.

05

Water Temperature Control

Discover how temperature from 88°C to 96°C dramatically changes your cup profile.

06

Sensory Tasting & Flavour Notes

Develop your palate — identify acidity, sweetness, body, and finish using a flavour wheel.
